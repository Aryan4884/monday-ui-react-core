## Keyframes

KeyFrames are the foundational building blocks of our motion system. They distinguish a component from being static. When KeyFrames are applied to components, they become a cohesive interactive system with a start and end state including intermediate waypoints.
To systematize these KeyFrames, We use a naming convention that describes them in bundles and describes them as animate-in and animate-out states

### Pop

The Pop KeaFrames significant property is the scale which delivers the feeling of a jump, pop-up

Use for component to appears. Focus the user
Use in System: Modals
